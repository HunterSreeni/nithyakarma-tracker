## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
Nithyakarma helps you keep up your daily anushtanams - Sandhyavandhanam,
parayanams, and japam - without losing track of what you've done.

SANDHYAVANDHANAM, DONE RIGHT
Mark all three sandhyas - Prathakala, Madhyanika, and Saayamkala - plus your
Gaayatri japam. The day only counts complete when all three are marked, just
like the real practice.

TRACK WHAT MATTERS TO YOUR HOUSEHOLD
Vishnu Sahasranamam, Lalitha Sahasranamam, Hanuman Chalisa, Narayaneeyam,
Bhagavad Gita, Aditya Hrudayam, Sri Rudram, Devi Mahatmyam, Soundarya Lahari,
Mukundamala, Subrahmanya Bhujangam, Dakshinamurthy Stotram, Bhagavatam
Parayanam, and more - daily, weekly, count-based, or sequence practices that
track chapter and dasakam progress on their own.

BUILD A STREAK THAT RESPECTS A MISSED DAY
A single missed day doesn't have to break months of practice - earn streak
freezes as you climb tiers, from Shishya to Brahmarishi, so one off day
doesn't erase your progress.

LEARN HANUMAN CHALISA, VERSE BY VERSE
Read and learn along in English, Malayalam, or Sanskrit, at your own pace.

KNOW TODAY'S PANCHANGAM AT A GLANCE
Thithi, nakshatram, the varsham name, and Rahu/Yamagandam/Gulika kalam
timings, right on your daily screen.

TRACK YOUR CHILDREN'S PRACTICE TOO
Add family members under 15 and mark their anushtanams on their behalf - each
child gets their own streak and progress, with an optional first-name-only
Bala Sabha leaderboard just for kids.

AN OPTIONAL COMMUNITY, ON YOUR TERMS
Turn on Sabha anytime to see how your streak and punya compare with others -
off by default, and your own row is never hidden from you even if you opt
out of showing others.

INVITE YOUR FAMILY AND FRIENDS
Share your invite link on WhatsApp - both of you get ad-free days and a
streak freeze when they join.

BUILT WITH CARE FOR YOUR DATA
Your account, and everything in it, can be deleted for real, anytime. We
never sell your data or share it with advertisers beyond what's needed to
show ads.

Nithyakarma is free to use. Built for the daily Sandhyavandhanam and
parayanam tradition, and open to anyone keeping up a daily practice.
```

## Notes for whoever submits this
- Short description is the line shown under the app name in search results and
  the store listing header - keep it feature-forward, not a tagline.
- Full description supports basic formatting in Play Console (line breaks,
  ALL-CAPS section headers as used above render fine; Play Console strips most
  other formatting).
- Reconcile any feature claims here against what's actually shipped before
  submitting - e.g. don't add karaoke/audio content language until Intent 2.1
  ships (currently only the Hanuman Chalisa text-only pilot, Intent 2.1a).
- Screenshots and feature graphic are tracked separately - see the rest of
  Intent 0.5 in `docs/UPGRADE-PLAN.md`.
